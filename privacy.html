Privacy Policy

Effective since June 17, 2026 · Developed by hanz (Nexalab) · Discord: kiko.lr

This Privacy Policy explains how AETHERIS BOT ("the Bot", "we") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with it on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide its features, the Bot collects:

  • Your Discord User ID and username
  • Server (Guild) ID and channel ID where commands are used
  • Gameplay and progression data: level, XP, virtual currency balance, bank balance, inventory items, badges, loot box history, fishing data, shop transactions, premium status, and transaction history
  • Ticket content submitted through the Open Ticket feature, for the purpose of support handling
  • Command usage logs (which slash commands are used and when), for debugging and improving features

If you log in to the Web Dashboard, we additionally receive the information authorized through Discord OAuth2 (such as your Discord ID, username, and avatar) needed to display your data and, for server admins, to manage admin tools and event settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to operate and maintain the Bot's features (leveling, economy, mini games, inventory, tickets, events, etc.), to respond to support requests, to detect and prevent abuse or exploitation of the Bot's systems, and to improve and develop new features over time.

3. Virtual Currency & Mini Games

All virtual currency, items, badges, and loot box contents tracked by the Bot exist only within the Bot's ecosystem and have no real-world monetary value. They cannot be exchanged, sold, or redeemed for real money or goods.

Mini games such as Dice, Coinflip, Slot, and Blackjack only use virtual currency for entertainment purposes and do not involve real-money transactions of any kind.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information only when required by law, to comply with a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of the Bot, its users, or the public.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's services, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8); once verified, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy & Age Requirement

The Bot is intended for use in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service, which sets a minimum age requirement for holding a Discord account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals who do not meet Discord's minimum age requirement.

8. Your Rights & Contact

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data associated with the Bot at any time by emailing raihan.ramandha@nexalab.my.id.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page. Continued use of the Bot after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
